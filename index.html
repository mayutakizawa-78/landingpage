About us

Would you like to share your academic research all over the world?

Anyone can easily upload videos on ScientistPage.

Scientific community is facing the exponential growth in academic literature. Researchers are publishing high quality research in journals and conferences, but most of the research papers goes uncited or undiscoverable. Our mission is to make the research work easily understandable and save the time invested by researchers in reading the complete papers to understand the concept.

We provide a platform to researchers (to present) and students (to watch) the research published in any journal or conference or even pre-published work in the video forms.

Our Story

ScientistPage is the platform of the researchers, by the researchers, for the researchers to exchange studies.

ScientistPage is the brainchild of founder, Shashank Tyagi. Doing research for his Masters and Ph.D, he realised that a lot of time of the researchers is wasted in reading the research papers and in most of the cases its very difficult to understand the concept by just reading. To solve this issue the founder comes with an idea to create a common platform where all authors can explain their research papers in videos and viewers can learn directly from them instead of reading. After 2 years of continuous research and feedback from the scientific societies and journals, ScientistPage was established as a legal entity in 2020. We are working with a vision to become the largest search engine for the videos of research papers, conferences and thesis which can save the most valuable time of the researchers and make the research work enjoyable.
